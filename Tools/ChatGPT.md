## Overview

ChatGPT is a generative artificial intelligence assistant developed by OpenAI that produces natural-language responses from user prompts. It assists with writing, summarization, brainstorming, coding, analysis, and educational tasks through conversational interactions.

Within cybersecurity, ChatGPT can support documentation, security awareness content, defensive guidance, scripting assistance, and simulated phishing exercises conducted in authorized environments.

---

## Key Features

- Natural language generation
- Context-aware conversations
- Content drafting and summarization
- Security awareness material creation
- Programming and scripting assistance
- Documentation support
- Brainstorming and research assistance
- Prompt-based interaction

---

## Common Usage

Typical workflow:

1. Open ChatGPT
2. Enter a prompt
3. Review the generated response
4. Refine the prompt if required
5. Use the generated content for the intended task

## CEH Practical Example

During **Module 09 – Social Engineering**, ChatGPT was used to generate realistic phishing email content for a controlled security awareness exercise. The AI-assisted prompts demonstrated how generative AI can rapidly produce convincing social engineering messages, highlighting the growing importance of employee awareness and phishing detection in defending against AI-assisted attacks.

---

## Defensive Perspective

Artificial intelligence can improve both offensive and defensive cybersecurity capabilities. Organizations should educate employees about AI-generated phishing content, strengthen email verification processes, implement multi-factor authentication, and conduct regular awareness training to reduce the effectiveness of AI-assisted social engineering attacks.
